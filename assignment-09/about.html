Abel Makkonen Testaye (The Weeknd) is a Canadian self-made singer-songwriter and record producer. Known for his sonic versatility and dark lyricism, his music explores escapism, romance, and melancholia inspired by his life experiences.

He has won three Grammy Awards, 19 Billboard Music Awards, 15 Juno Awards, six American Music Awards, two MTV Video Music Awards, and an Academy Award nomination.

Testaye cites Michael Jackson, Prince, and R. Kelly as his main musical inspirations. His high-flying vocal style was influenced by Ethiopian singers such as Aster Aweke. He grew up listening to many different genres, including soul, hip hop, funk, indie rock, and post-punk, and is heavely influenced by 1980s music.
